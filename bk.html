66 Laws HoI

  • There is a light within you, and the world did not give it to you. You must let the light within you shine.

  • Show love to those that love you and take care of those that have taken care of you.

  • Respect your haters - Acknowledge them, thank them, and rise to greater hights. Love your haters and wish good upon those who do you harm.

  • Make plans - Write your dreams into reality.

  • Always be a man of your word.

  • If a compromise is required to save your family or the House, then consider it.

  • Pay your debts.

  • Preserve life at all costs. Don't waiste bullets.

  • The House will only promote those that have studied their craft and are ready for the next level.

  • The art of compromise should be skillfully employed so that you always keep the advantage. Negotiate with your best intrest in mind.

  • Guard your light and protect it from self harm. Your body is a temple for the light.

  • Honor your mother and father for their light brought you into the light. You honor the Greater Light when you respect your parents.

  • Know yourself and your enemies - Learn both your ways and their ways. Discover your upper and lower limits.

  • Know your strengths and your weaknesses - Surround yourself with those wjose light compliments your weaknesses.

  • Their can only be one boss, and their can only be one head of the house.

  • Know when to sign the contract and when to walk away.

  • Remember that everything good, is not always good for you.

  • Don't sell or prostitute your light. Don't lose your light, or your sould, for fame and fortune.

  • What happens in the House stays in the House.

  • Do not seek fame. Instead seek to let your light shine and fame and forutne will follow.

  • Never back a man into a corner unless you are ready to fight.

  • Money, Power, Respect - of these three, respect is the most important. Self respect being the greatest of the respects.

  • Always be humble.

  • Remember that the young shall one day be old.

  • Make offers that can not be refused. Tell people what you want, polietly. He who asks is he who recieves.

  • Always be fair. Be just and seek justice where justice is due.

  • Take care of your children and teach your children in the ways of the light and encourage them to be the best at whatever talents have been gifted them.
  • Make things right when you have commited a wrong.


  • Observe more, speak less, and learn more.

  • Don't put everything on the table. Save some, just in case.

  • If its too good to be true, most likely it is too good to be true.
  • You will alaways have 2 choices: Peace or War. If you have to choose, always choose peace. War leads to destruction of the light and the family. If you must enter war, do so with the clear aim of achieving peace.

  • Revenge will destroy the light and weaken the family. Revenge should always be avoided, but if it comes down to it, then revenge must be balanced with justice. But in the house, the only revenge is success!

  • Continued success requires continued improvement. Some things must be earned with a slow determination. Too much too soon can be deadly. A captain must earn their rank.

  • First and formost, acknowledge God, the creator of the light - in all things that you do. God is the supreme leader of the children of the light.     

  • When shit gets real, stay calm. Remain calm in all situations.                                                                                                                                                                 
  • Your life must mean something, otherwise your living will be in vain.                                                                                                                                                                
  • Be watchful of the time, for there are a limited number of hours in the day. Always be on time to your appointed place.

  • Don't be afraind to go the desert road alone. Travel Lightly.

  • Keep your appearance decent in public at all times.

  • Take care of your own house before you take care of the houses of others - make your bed in the morning.

  • Don't preced your spoken words with "let me be honest" or "real talk".

  • You should never make assumptions.

  • Give your time to those things which are inmportant.

  • In order to get the work done, you must show up.

  • Learn how to live with rejection. Rejection is not always defeat.

  • Learn to say "okay" convincingly - it is not the most important thing to win an argument...Sometimes you gain more in defeat.

  • Shake handes, look people in the eye. You can tell a lot about a man from his grip.

  • You should know your craft like the back of your hand.

  • Remain selfless in serving others.

  • Make excellence a habit. After the rest of the team finishes its grind, you must continue grinding.

  • Rise early in the morning and work late into the night.

  • Never be afraid to try and never be afraid to fail.

  • Talk to people as if you have known them all your life.

  • The people behind the scenes have name - learn their names because they are just as important as the ones who are out front.

  • Learn to control your impulses and emotions - be slow to anger.

  • Surround yourself with those who are walking in the path of the light. Successful people don't hang around broke people.

  • Provide no excuses. Provide an explanation only if it is required, but do offer an appology if the fault or error is found to be yours.

  • Men should always respect women for they are the vessel in which new light is created. When you seek a mate, find the highest quality vessel to bear the light.

  • Beware of the watchers and keep a close eye on your enemy.

  • Accept the qualities of a person, even the bad ones, when they show you who they are.

  • Know more than one way home. Mix it up. Take different routes/paths.

  • Use the light to make decisions - Be decisive when the time comes.

  • Tell no lies and do not share the secrets of others.

  • Pursue the calling of your light - chase your passions and live your dreams for the Greater Light requires this.

  • Keep these Laws, these Lessons and your commitments for there are consequences to breaking the Laws, Lessons, and your commitments. Write the Laws upon your heart. Live as if your life depended on them. The path to success is guaranteed if you keep the Laws.
